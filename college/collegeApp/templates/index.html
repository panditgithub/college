Our Popular Courses

GNM - General Nursing & Midwifery

A 3-year professional course focused on clinical nursing, patient care, and hospital management.

A 2-year course preparing frontline health workers, with a focus on maternal and child care.

Specialized training in surgical assistance, equipment handling, and OT procedures.
